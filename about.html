About Us

All In Brewing is a craft home brewing operation based out of Floyds Knobs Indiana. Named for our brewing style - bold, and willing to take risks which can be uncomfortable but also delivers better results than playing it safe, and our other passion - poker.

In the brewery, Director of Brewing Operations Jeff Uligian's beer program consists of an ever-changing lineup, with styles ranging from dry hopped IPAs to belgian tripels and funk-forward saisons.

Our beers are available on draft at the brewery in Southern Indiana, and to-go in bottle, 32oz crowlers and 64oz growlers.
