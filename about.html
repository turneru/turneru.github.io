About Us

All In Brewing is a craft home brewery based out of Floyds Knobs Indiana, named for our passion to lay it all on the line and our second love after beer - poker.

In the brewery, Director of Brewing Operations Jeff Uligian's beer program consists of an ever-changing lineup, with styles ranging from dry hopped IPAs to sweet stouts and funk-forward saisons.

Our beers are only available at the brewery in Southern Indiana, and to-go in 32oz crowlers and 64oz growlers.
